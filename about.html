About Us

Alichin Hair Care Platform

Alichin is a digital-first hair care startup based in the Netherlands, focused on transforming how people understand and care for their hair. We combine e-commerce with personalized hair insights to deliver smarter, data-driven recommendations tailored to each user.

Instead of generic product shopping, we help users build structured hair care routines, track progress over time, and receive intelligent suggestions based on their hair type, environment, and goals.

Our platform is designed to make hair care more scientific, personalized, and effective—bridging the gap between beauty products and real results.
